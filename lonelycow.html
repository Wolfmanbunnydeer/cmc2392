Lonely Cow

The Lonely Cow is one of the painting artwork showing a lonely bovine herbivore or male calf friend to play with who live on a farm.

The male calf in the artwork is begging other farm animals to play with him.

After all, male calves find new friends no matter their species. Now the calf is joy in a tear that he finally has friends in the blessing of the heart of no more loneliness!!!
